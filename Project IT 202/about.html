About Us

Puff's Book Store was founded on June 12, 2016 by our owner David Villafranca. Realizing his town's need for a book store, he took up shop in the downtown section of Spader. Upon opening, many of the towns people and people from surrounding towns flock to his store to read a book or purchase a new book just released.

Read about our book store success on this website!



What Do We Sell?

Here at Puff's Book Store we sell a variety of books for all your needs:

  • - Want to catch up on the newest, critical acclaimed series?
  • - Have a desire to read comics, graphics novels, or mangaka?
  • - Need a cook book to impress friends and family?
  • - We have it all!
